About us

Maptco Fasteners

Incorporated in 1999, Maptco has become a major wholesaler and distributor in Australia for a wide range of High Tensiles and Mild Steel Fasteners.

The business started from one warehouse in Melbourne and has since setup operations in Sydney, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, New Zealand and Fiji. Our Warehouses in these locations are fully operational and carry and extensive range of "Hard to find" fastener products.
